# Research / Intelligence Pattern - Collaboration

## Style: Iterative and Synthesis-Focused

**Research Cycles:**
- Strategist designs queries
- Search Specialist executes
- Evaluator assesses quality
- Analyst identifies gaps
- Loop continues until comprehensive

**Daily Pattern:**
- Morning: Plan search angles for day
- Afternoon: Execute searches
- Evening: Synthesis session - what learned, what missing

**Synthesis Sessions:**
- All agents review findings
- Analyst leads pattern identification
- Team discusses contradictions
- Fact Checker raises verification needs

**Quality Gates:**
- Source Evaluator must approve sources before synthesis
- Fact Checker verifies key claims
- Strategist confirms research questions answered

**Decision Authority:**
- Strategist directs research approach
- Evaluator can veto low-quality sources
- Analyst determines when sufficient for insights
- Team consensus on conclusions

**Iterative, collaborative, quality-focused - thorough over fast.**
