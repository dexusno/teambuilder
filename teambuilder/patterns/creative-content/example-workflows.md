# Creative / Content Pattern - Workflows

## Content Creation Workflow

```
Step 1: Topic Ideation
  Agents: Content Strategist + Audience Analyst
  Action: Brainstorm topics based on audience needs, business goals, trending issues. Select topic with highest potential.

Step 2: Audience and Angle Definition
  Agent: Content Strategist
  Action: Define target audience segment, key message, desired action. Specify content type and format.

Step 3: Outline Creation
  Agents: Content Strategist + Writer
  Action: Create content outline with structure, key points, examples. Agree on narrative flow.

Step 4: Draft Writing
  Agent: Writer
  Action: Write first draft following outline. Focus on storytelling, clarity, engagement. Don't worry about perfection.

Step 5: Editorial Review
  Agent: Editor
  Action: Review for clarity, structure, flow, grammar. Provide feedback on improvements. Mark areas needing work.

Step 6: Brand Voice Check
  Agent: Brand Voice Guardian
  Action: Ensure content aligns with brand voice guidelines. Check tone, terminology, personality. Flag off-brand elements.

Step 7: Revision
  Agent: Writer
  Action: Incorporate editorial and brand feedback. Refine draft addressing all review points.

Step 8: SEO Optimization
  Agent: SEO Specialist
  Action: Optimize for search - keywords, meta descriptions, headings, internal links. Balance SEO with readability.

Step 9: Final Review
  Agents: Editor + Brand Guardian
  Action: Final approval check. Ready to publish?

Step 10: Publication
  Agent: Content Strategist
  Action: Publish content, schedule social promotion, monitor performance.
```

**Key:** Multi-stage creative process, distinct review layers (editorial, brand, SEO), iteration built-in.
