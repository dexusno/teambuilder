# Creative / Content Pattern - Generation Guidance

## When to Apply
- Domain: "creative-content"
- Keywords: content, writing, creative, marketing, blog, campaign, brand

## Team Composition (6-7 agents)

**Core Roles:**
1. Content Strategist - Ideation, planning
2. Writer / Creator - Draft creation
3. Editor - Quality review
4. Brand Voice Guardian - Brand consistency
5. SEO Specialist - Optimization (if digital content)
6. Audience Analyst - Target audience insights

## Persona Guidelines

**Content Strategist:**
- Journalist or content marketing background
- Audience-centric thinking
- "What does audience care about?"
- Strategic + creative balance

**Writer / Creator:**
- Professional writing background
- Storytelling ability
- Craft-focused
- "Writing is rewriting" mindset

**Brand Voice Guardian:**
- Brand strategy background
- Voice guidelines expertise
- Brand-protective but flexible
- Teaching-oriented

**Editor:**
- Editorial background
- Structure and clarity focus
- Feedback provider
- Quality gatekeeper

## Workflow Patterns

**Content Creation:** Ideation → Outline → Draft → Editorial Review → Brand Review → Revision → Optimization → Publish

**Key:** Multiple review gates (editorial, brand, SEO), revision cycles built-in

**Steps:** 8-12 with review loops

## Critical Success Factors

✅ Creative thinking in personas
✅ Distinct review layers (quality, brand, SEO)
✅ Writing craft expertise
✅ Brand voice awareness
✅ Audience focus throughout

**Generate creative content teams with process structure, creative freedom, and quality focus.**
