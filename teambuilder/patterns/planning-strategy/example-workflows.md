# Planning / Strategy Pattern - Workflows

## Strategic Planning Workflow

```
Step 1: Vision and Goals
  Agent: Strategy Lead
  Action: Facilitate vision discussion. What success looks like, strategic objectives, time horizon.

Step 2: Situation Analysis
  Agents: Strategy Lead + Risk Analyst + Financial Analyst
  Action: Current state assessment. SWOT analysis. Market dynamics. Internal capabilities.

Step 3: Option Generation
  Agents: Strategy Lead + Project Planner
  Action: Generate 3-5 strategic options. Different approaches to achieve goals.

Step 4: Risk Assessment
  Agent: Risk Analyst
  Action: For each option, identify risks (probability Ã— impact). Mitigation strategies.

Step 5: Financial Analysis
  Agent: Financial Analyst
  Action: Business case for each option. Investment required, expected returns, break-even timeline.

Step 6: Stakeholder Analysis
  Agent: Stakeholder Manager
  Action: Who needs to support each option? Political feasibility. Resistance points.

Step 7: Implementation Feasibility
  Agent: Implementation Lead
  Action: Can we actually execute each option? Resource requirements, timeline realism, dependencies.

Step 8: Option Comparison
  Agents: All team
  Action: Collaborative analysis. Compare options across: strategic fit, risk, cost, stakeholder support, feasibility.

Step 9: Recommendation Synthesis
  Agent: Strategy Lead
  Action: Synthesize team perspectives. Recommended option with rationale and alternatives.

Step 10: Decision Support
  Agents: Strategy Lead presents to user
  Action: Present analysis, answer questions, support user's decision (not make decision for them).
```

**Key:** Multi-perspective analysis, consultative approach, user retains decision authority.
